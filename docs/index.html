WHMCS compatibility reference

Copy-paste-safe emoji for client-facing WHMCS content.

Search, click, and copy symbols that are more likely to survive WHMCS product names, templates, emails, announcements, and knowledge base pages without turning into broken characters.

0 emoji

Usually safer

  • Basic symbols and older Unicode emoji
  • Simple single-codepoint emoji
  • Emoji without skin tone modifiers
  • Emoji without gender or family combinations

Often unsupported

  • Country flags
  • New emoji from recent Unicode releases
  • Skin tone variations
  • Complex zero-width-joiner emoji sequences
